Q4/- What are the different types of Maven repositories?


Maven Repository:
================

*** A repository is storage where maven maintains plugins, archetypes, and lots of jar files used for building different kinds of java projects.

maven repo is of 3 types:

1. Central repo:- it is the maven's own repo in which it maintains all kinds of project-related plugins, archetypes, jars, etc.

https://repo1.maven.org/maven2.


2. Remote repo: this repo is maintained within the organization for sharing plugins, archetypes, and jar files for multiple projects within 
  
 organizations. ex: masairepo.



3. Local repo: this repo will be created inside the developer's computer (.m2) is the name of this repo.
